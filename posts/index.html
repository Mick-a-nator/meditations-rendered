Make Mistakes

When you are afraid of failure or making mistakes you become inactive, however the only true failure is never having started. You absorb yourself it what is “best”: Best practices, best frameworks, best theories. For all the research in the world, there will always be something you have missed, something unique to you and your situation. Your perfect plan will crumble and you will have to adapt. And you will adapt, but at what cost?
